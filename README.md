## What Is SoftShellWeb?

SoftShellWeb (registered as SOFTSHELL HOSTING LTD in England and Wales) is a direct infrastructure operator — meaning they own their own hardware and aren't reselling someone else's servers. That's actually a bigger deal than it sounds, because it means you're not playing telephone with support when something goes wrong.

They've been quietly delivering hosting since 2019, and in that time they've expanded from Amsterdam into Utah (USA), Los Angeles (California), Taiwan, and China-optimized routes. Their flagship product is KVM VPS hosting, though they also offer shared hosting and reseller plans.
